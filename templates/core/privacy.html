For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, “iSellTZ,” “we,” “us,” or “our” refers to Genie for the new world, of Dar es salaam .

  • Sign in using Google;
  • Publish or respond to a listing;
  • Apply for account or business verification;
  • Communicate with another user or with iSellTZ; or
  • Otherwise interact with the Platform.

We process personal data in accordance with applicable laws of the United Republic of Tanzania, including the Personal Data Protection Act, 2022, its regulations, and other applicable legal requirements.

3. Personal Data We Collect

The personal data we collect depends on how you use iSellTZ.

3.1 Account and profile information

When you create or manage an account, we may collect:

  • Your full name;
  • Email address;
  • Telephone number;
  • Profile image;
  • Account type or marketplace role;
  • Business, agency, dealership, landlord, retailer, or professional information;
  • Account status and verification status;
  • Login and account-security information; and
  • Any additional profile information you voluntarily provide.

3.2 Information received through Google Sign-In

When you choose “Continue with Google” or otherwise use Google Sign-In, we may receive the following information from Google, depending on the permissions shown to you:

  • Your name;
  • Your email address;
  • Your Google profile image;
  • A unique Google account identifier; and
  • Information needed to confirm that your Google account has been authenticated.

We use this information only to create, identify, secure, and manage your iSellTZ account and to provide the services you request.

iSellTZ does not receive or store your Google password.

Unless a separate feature clearly asks for additional permission and you expressly approve it, iSellTZ does not access your Gmail messages, Google Drive files, Google contacts, Google Calendar, photographs stored in Google services, or other private Google account content.

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including applicable Limited Use requirements.

You may disconnect iSellTZ from your Google account through your Google account permissions. Disconnecting Google access does not necessarily delete information already stored in your iSellTZ account. To delete that information, you must also request deletion of your iSellTZ account.

3.3 Listing information

When you create a listing, we may collect and publish information such as:

  • Listing title and description;
  • Price or expected price;
  • Product, property, vehicle, or service category;
  • Photographs, videos, or other uploaded media;
  • General or precise listing location;
  • Property characteristics;
  • Vehicle characteristics;
  • Business or product details;
  • Ownership, authorisation, or verification information;
  • Contact details selected for publication; and
  • Any other information included in the listing.

Information included in a public listing may be viewed, copied, shared, indexed, or stored by other users, search engines, and third parties. You should not publish sensitive information that is unnecessary for a marketplace listing.

3.4 Verification information

Where iSellTZ provides account, landlord, retailer, dealer, agent, or business verification, we may request:

  • Identity information;
  • Business registration or licence information;
  • Proof of authority to advertise a listing;
  • Proof of ownership or control;
  • Contact verification information;
  • Photographs or copies of supporting documents; and
  • Results of verification reviews.

Verification information may contain sensitive personal data. Where required, we will obtain appropriate consent and apply additional safeguards.

3.5 Communications

We may collect information contained in:

  • Messages or enquiries sent through the Platform;
  • Reports about listings or users;
  • Support requests;
  • Complaints and appeals;
  • Feedback and survey responses;
  • Emails, telephone calls, or other communications with us; and
  • Notifications and communication preferences.

Where users communicate outside iSellTZ using telephone numbers, email addresses, messaging applications, or external platforms, those communications are controlled by the relevant users and external service providers.

3.6 Technical and usage information

When you access iSellTZ, we may automatically collect:

  • Internet Protocol address;
  • Browser and device type;
  • Operating system;
  • Device identifiers;
  • Login dates and times;
  • Pages viewed and features used;
  • Referring and exit pages;
  • Error records;
  • Security and fraud-detection logs;
  • Cookie and session information; and
  • Approximate location inferred from an IP address.

This information helps us maintain security, diagnose technical problems, prevent misuse, and improve the Platform.

3.7 Location information

A user may voluntarily provide the location of a property, vehicle, business, or other listing.

Where a map or device-location feature is used, the user may be asked for permission before precise device location is accessed. You may deny or withdraw device-location permission through your browser or device settings, although some location features may then be unavailable.
